Our Story

AI should be free.
We're here to prove it.

We got tired of AI tool directories that mix free and paid tools together — expired free tiers, tools that require a credit card, and low-quality spam propped up by SEO.

So we built JustFreeAI — an AI for everything, all with genuine free tiers. Not a "7-day trial." Not "free with a credit card." Actually free.

Our Curation Rules

✦
Genuine free tier

The tool must have a real free tier — not a trial, not a free tier that requires a credit card to activate.

✦
Real quality & positive user feedback

We check reviews, forums, and user sentiment before listing. No broken, buggy, or poorly-reviewed tools.

✦
Zero spam / zero dark patterns

Tools that aggressively upsell, spam users, or use manipulative design are immediately removed.

✦
Solves a real problem

We only list tools that meaningfully help real people with real challenges — not novelty demos.

How we make money

We're transparent about this. JustFreeAI earns revenue through:

  • Display advertising — standard ad slots on the site. We never let ad networks choose which tools are listed.
  • Sponsored listings — tools can pay for a "Sponsored" placement. All sponsored tools are clearly labeled and must still pass our curation rules.

We will never accept payment to change a tool's rating, write fake reviews, or list a tool that doesn't meet our free-tier standard.
